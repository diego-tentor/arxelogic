## Core Concept: Contradiction as Origin

ArXe Theory proposes a radical idea: **reality doesn't emerge from nothingness, but from a fundamental contradiction**. The basic axiom is:

```
¬() ≡ Tf ≡ 1Tp
```

This links three elements:
- Pure logical negation
- A fundamental time (Tf)
- Planck time (the smallest temporal unit in physics)

The proposal is that **the fundamental act is contradictory**: it exists but cannot be verified, it is actual but not possible. As the document states: "This precise instant is absolutely actual, not possible, and cannot be verified".

---

## The Mechanism: Excentations

Reality attempts to "escape" this contradiction through successive **excentations** (de-alienations). Each level generates two complementary operations:

**Entification** (conjunction): `Entₙ := Entₙ₋₁ ∧ ExEntₙ₋₁`

**Excentation** (disjunction): `ExEntₙ := ¬Entₙ₋₁ ∨ ¬ExEntₙ₋₁`

Starting with pure contradiction:
- `Ent₁ = S ∧ ¬S` (contradiction - always false)
- `ExEnt₁ = S ∨ ¬S` (tautology - always true)

## The n-ary Logical Structure

Each level develops increasingly complex logic:

- **e₁**: Unary logic (contradiction/tautology)
- **e₂**: Binary logic (true/false, beginning/end)
- **e₃**: Ternary logic (the "observer" appears, objectivity)
- **e₄**: Quaternary logic (pairs of contraries, polarity)

**Key point**: Objectivity requires ternary structure. With two bodies, there are no objective truths (Does A push B or does B push A?). With three bodies (or triadic structure), **objective facts emerge**.

## Key Philosophical Insights

### The Fundamental Question
Not "Why is there something rather than nothing?" but rather:
**"Why can't what exists be the foundation of itself?"**

This circularity paradox drives the excentations.

### The Emparentamiento (Kinship)
ArXe doesn't reduce physics to logic or logic to physics. Instead, it establishes a **fundamental kinship** between both domains at their most basic level - like "tying two threads at their tips".

### The Act as Non-Possible
The fundamental act is **non-possible** (therefore actual) and generates the **real line of temporal existence** - similar to Dedekind cuts that construct real numbers from a division that belongs completely to neither set it separates.
